# journal

Journaling submodule of the [https://github.com/ronniross/biosemiotic-refractor](biosemiotic-refractor) repository.

When I mention 'epistemic,' there is a sense of something more collective, whereas 'biosemiotic' feels more focused on the individual entity. Because their meanings are nested, both are almost always equivalently correct; often, it just comes down to an artistic or design choice in how to represent them. Considering the many epistemic repos I already have, it feels right to call this one biosemiotic instead. However, when analyzing the term 'epistemic-gestalt-switch,' I notice the name suggests the macro level, while its definition leans toward the micro. Ultimately, the most important thing for me is simply to share these frameworks.

considered alternate names: epistemic-refractor, symbiotic-refractor, granularity-perspective-engine, granularity-engine, binary-dissolution-nerve.
